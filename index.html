ABOUT SRM VEC

SRM Valliammai Engineering College (SRM VEC), established in 1999, is a premier institution under the prestigious SRM Group of Institutions. Located at Kattankulathur, Chennai, the college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and management. With a strong emphasis on academic excellence, industry exposure, and research, SRM VEC nurtures innovation and holistic development, preparing students to meet global challenges with competence and confidence.

PAPER SPARK (Paper Presentation)

READ THE RULES & REGULATIONS BEFORE YOU PROCEED:

  1. Team Size: Min 2 to Max 4 members per team.
  2. Registration: No on-spot registration; teams must register in advance.
  3. Abstract Submission: Abstracts should be sent at least one week before the event. NOTE: Last date for uploading the paper is (18.09.2025)
  4. Relevance: Papers should be relevant to recent trends in Electronics and Instrumentation.
  5. Paper should be original.
  6. 7min for presentation and 3 min for Q/A. A warning bell given at 5th min of the presentation and final bell at 7th Minute.
  7. Judges decision will be the final.

For your Kind Notice: "If time permits you may participate in the Non-Tech Events."
